# Cocolate Cookies

## Ingredients

- 125 g butter, softened
- 150 g sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 120 g all-purpose flour
- 40 g unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt
- Optional: 80–100 g chocolate chunks or chips for extra richness


## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a bowl, beat together the butter and sugar until creamy.
3. Mix in the egg and vanilla until fully combined.
4. Add the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Stir until a smooth dough forms.
5. (Optional) Fold in chocolate chunks for extra chocolatey cookies.
6. Scoop small balls of dough (about 1 tbsp each) onto the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 8–10 minutes — they should still look a bit soft in the center.
8. Let cool on the tray for 5 minutes before transferring to a rack.
